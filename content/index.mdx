# Noctune Docs

Noctune turns what you say during an appointment into a structured SOAP note. Record on your phone or in the browser, and a draft is waiting for you by the time the patient leaves the room.

This site walks through every part of the app in the order you'll encounter it in the sidebar.

## Start here

- [Getting Started](/guides/getting-started) — sign up, accept terms, first login
- [Dashboard](/guides/dashboard) — the landing page and quick actions
- [Encounters](/guides/encounters) — recording, reviewing, and editing SOAP notes

## By workflow

- [Patients](/guides/patients) — patient records and visit history
- [Templates](/guides/templates) — customize how SOAP notes are structured
- [Messages](/guides/messages) — vet-to-vet correspondence
- [Practice](/guides/practice) — team and practice settings
- [Settings](/guides/settings) — account, billing, and notifications
- [Community](/guides/community) — shared templates from other vets
