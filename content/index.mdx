# Noctune Docs

**You see the patient. We handle the rest.**

Noctune is the AI for veterinary medicine — not just a scribe. Record a visit on your iOS device, and Noctune writes the SOAP note, drafts the follow-up, flags the tough conversation, and keeps a secure audio record of every encounter.

## What Noctune does

* **Writes your SOAP notes.** Structured output in under a minute, with speaker labels and timestamps.
* **Drafts client messages.** Discharge instructions, follow-ups, and visit summaries — sent through Noctune, encrypted, tied to the patient record.
* **Flags tough conversations.** Frustration, confusion, or tension during a visit gets marked on your dashboard, before it shows up in a bad review.
* **Keeps the audio.** Every visit is recorded and retrievable — replay the exact moment you prescribed something or refresh before a follow-up.
* **Catches what got missed.** Surfaces relevant context from prior visits, suggests differentials, flags things the client said that didn't make it into the note.
* **Works in a real exam room.** Your phone is the microphone. Handles barking, clippers, and the dental scaler in the next room.
