# New Encounter

Start a new encounter from the **New Encounter** pill button in the sidebar or the Dashboard. You can either record live or upload a pre-recorded file.

![New Encounter entry screen with Record and Upload buttons](https://placecats.com/900/500)

## Recording live

1. Click **Record**. Grant microphone permission the first time.
2. Select the patient (or create one on the fly — see [Patients](/guides/patients)).
3. Pick a [template](/guides/templates) if you want the SOAP note formatted a specific way.
4. Talk through the appointment. The timer shows elapsed time; the level meter shows you're being heard.
5. Click **Stop** when the appointment ends.

![Active recording screen with waveform, elapsed timer, and Stop button](https://placecats.com/900/460)

> **Tip:** You can pause and resume — pauses don't add to the audio file, so there's no dead air in the transcript.

## Uploading audio

If you recorded with another app (phone voice memo, dictaphone, etc.), upload the file instead. MP3, M4A, WAV, and most common audio formats work.

![Upload dialog showing supported audio formats and file selection area](https://placecats.com/900/420)

## What happens next

Once audio is submitted, Noctune transcribes it and drafts a SOAP note. This typically takes a minute or two. When it's ready the encounter moves from **Processing** to **Draft** and you can open it for review.

![Encounter card in Processing state with progress indicator](https://placecats.com/700/220)
