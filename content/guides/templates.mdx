# Templates

Templates control how Noctune formats a SOAP note. You can use the built-in defaults, customize them, or build your own from scratch.

![Templates page with system templates, my templates, and community library sections](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## System vs. user templates

- **System templates** are the Noctune-provided defaults (Wellness Exam, Emergency, Dental, etc.). You can't edit them, but you can duplicate one and modify the copy.
- **My templates** are yours. Edit, reorder, and delete freely.

![Side-by-side view of a system template and a user-owned template with edit controls](https://placecats.com/900/440)

## Creating a template

Click **New Template**. A template is mostly a structured prompt — you describe each section (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) and any conventions you want followed (e.g. "use metric weights," "bullet Plan items").

![New template editor with section blocks for Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan](https://placecats.com/900/520)

## Reordering

Drag templates by the handle on the left to change their order in the picker that appears during recording. The top template becomes your default.

![Template list with a drag handle visible on a row mid-drag](https://placecats.com/900/300)

## Duplicating

Every template (yours or a system template) has a **Duplicate** action. The copy lands in **My Templates** and is fully editable.

![Three-dot menu on a template showing Duplicate, Edit, and Delete options](https://placecats.com/700/300)
