# Settings

Settings is split into three tabs. Pick the area you need.

![Settings page with tabs for Account, Billing & Credits, and Notifications](https://placecats.com/960/420)

## In this section

- [Account](/guides/settings/account) — profile, email, password
- [Billing & Credits](/guides/settings/billing) — plans, usage, payment methods, promo codes
- [Notifications](/guides/settings/notifications) — email and in-app preferences

> Looking for team management? That lives under [Practice](/guides/practice), not Settings.
