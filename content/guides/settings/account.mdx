# Account

The Account tab is your personal profile — name, email, and password. Nothing here affects your practice or billing.

![Account settings tab with profile photo, name, email, and password change fields](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## Profile

Your name and photo appear on messages and in the team roster. Click the avatar to upload or change the photo.

![Profile card with avatar upload, display name, and role](https://placecats.com/900/360)

## Email

Changing your email requires confirming the new address via a verification link. Until you confirm, you can still sign in with the old one.

![Email change form with current email, new email, and verification status](https://placecats.com/900/300)

## Password

If you signed up with Google or Apple, you don't have a password yet — click **Set password** to create one. This is useful if you want email/password as a backup sign-in method.

![Password change form with current password, new password, and confirm fields](https://placecats.com/900/360)

## Logging out

The **Log out** button at the bottom signs you out everywhere. If you suspect your account was accessed from a device you don't own, use this and then change your password.
