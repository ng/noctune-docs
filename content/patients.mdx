# Patients

The Patients page is your list of every animal you've seen. Each patient row links to a profile with vitals, history, and every past encounter.

![Patients list page with search, species filter, and Add Patient button](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## Adding a patient

Click **Add Patient** in the top right. Name, species, breed, and owner are the only required fields — the rest can be filled in later (or auto-filled from a recording).

![Add patient dialog with name, species, breed, sex, date of birth, and owner fields](https://placecats.com/900/520)

## Patient profile

Open a patient to see everything about them in one place:

- **Demographics** — name, species, breed, age, sex, microchip
- **Vitals** — weight, temperature, heart rate trends across visits
- **History** — every encounter tied to this patient, newest first

![Patient profile page with demographics card, vital trend charts, and encounter history list](https://placecats.com/1000/560)

## Prior visit context

When you start a new encounter for an existing patient, Noctune pulls relevant context from prior visits into the SOAP note's drafting — so ongoing conditions and medications show up in the assessment without you having to dictate them again.

![Prior visit context panel showing recent diagnoses and active medications](https://placecats.com/900/360)

## Editing and deleting

Every field on the profile is editable inline. The three-dot menu in the header has **Delete Patient** — this also removes the tie from past encounters but leaves the encounters themselves intact.

![Patient header with three-dot action menu open showing Edit and Delete options](https://placecats.com/700/260)
