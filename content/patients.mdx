# Patients

Patient Records brings each animal's encounters and extracted clinical trends together. Use **All Patients**, **Active**, or **Recent**, and search by patient or owner details.

## Add a patient

Choose **Add Patient**, enter the patient name, and confirm. This lightweight form is designed for quick capture; the current web dialog does not ask for species, breed, sex, birth date, or owner contact.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/screenshots/patients/add-patient.webp"
  alt="Add Patient dialog with a required patient name field"
  url="app.noctune.ai/patients"
/>

You can also create a patient from the selector in the New Encounter drawer.

## Patient profile

The profile header summarizes available species and breed information, encounter count, last visit, and status. When an owner email is on file, **Email owner** opens a pre-addressed message.

Vitals discussed in encounters appear as trends for weight, temperature, heart rate, respiratory rate, and body-condition score. These are AI-extracted values, so verify them against the transcript before making a clinical decision.

The **Encounter History** section below the trends lists the patient's visits and opens the underlying record.

## Prior-visit context during review

An encounter's **Past Encounters** tab keeps the patient's earlier clinical summaries next to the current note. Search the history, expand a visit, or open the full prior encounter in a new tab.

## Rename or remove a patient

Use the pencil action in the list or profile to rename a patient. The trash action in the list opens **Remove Patient**. Removal archives the patient so it no longer appears in the active list; existing transcripts and SOAP notes are not deleted.
