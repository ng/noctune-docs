# Practice

A **practice** is the clinic you work at. Setting one up lets you share templates and patients across a team and bill from a single account.

![Practice page with practice profile card, team members table, and invite button](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## Practice profile

The profile holds the clinic name, address, phone, and logo. The logo appears on discharge notes and any PDFs you export.

![Practice profile edit form with name, address, phone, and logo upload field](https://placecats.com/900/480)

## Team members

The team table lists everyone with access. Admin members can invite, change roles, and remove members. Regular members can only change their own account settings.

![Team members table with name, email, role dropdown, and remove button per row](https://placecats.com/900/420)

### Inviting a member

Click **Invite**, enter an email, and pick a role. They'll get an invite email; once accepted, they appear in the team list with access to practice-level templates and patients.

![Invite member dialog with email input and role selector (Admin vs. Member)](https://placecats.com/800/380)

### Roles

- **Admin** — manage members, billing, and practice settings
- **Member** — record encounters, view team patients, manage own account

## Leaving or transferring

If you're the only admin and need to leave, promote another member to admin first. Contact support if you need to transfer ownership between practices.
