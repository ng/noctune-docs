# Getting Started

Use Noctune in a browser to record an appointment, upload existing audio, review the transcript, and finish the clinical note. The iOS app can also capture audio and send the encounter to the same workspace.

## 1. Sign up or sign in

Open the sign-in page. You can continue with Google or Apple, or choose **Sign in with email instead** to use an email address and password.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/screenshots/getting-started/sign-in.webp"
  alt="Noctune sign-in page with Google, Apple, and email sign-in options"
  url="app.noctune.ai/sign-in"
/>

If a practice invited you, sign in with the address that received the invitation.

## 2. Accept the terms

The first time you sign in, review the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. **Accept & Continue** becomes available after you select both acknowledgements. Noctune asks again only when your account has not accepted the current versions.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/screenshots/getting-started/accept-terms.webp"
  alt="Noctune legal acceptance page with the current Terms of Service and two acknowledgement checkboxes"
  url="app.noctune.ai/accept-terms"
/>

## 3. Land on the dashboard

After acceptance, Noctune opens the Dashboard. It brings the selected day's encounters, recent client replies, outstanding work, activity calendar, and recording shortcut into one view.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/screenshots/dashboard/overview.webp"
  alt="Noctune dashboard with encounters, recent messages, work summary, calendar, and start encounter shortcut"
  url="app.noctune.ai/dashboard"
/>
