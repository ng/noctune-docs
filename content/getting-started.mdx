# Getting Started

This guide covers creating your account and landing on the dashboard for the first time. Recording itself happens on your iOS device — this walkthrough gets you to the point where you're ready to record.

## 1. Sign up or sign in

Visit the sign-in page and either create an account or log in with an existing one. Email/password, Google, and Apple are all supported.

![Noctune sign-in page showing email, Google, and Apple sign-in options](https://placecats.com/900/500)

> **Tip:** If you signed up through your practice, use the same email your admin invited.

## 2. Accept the terms

The first time you sign in, you'll be asked to accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. You only do this once per account.

![Accept terms screen with checkboxes for Terms of Service and Privacy Policy](https://placecats.com/900/400)

## 3. Land on the dashboard

After accepting terms you're dropped on the Dashboard. This is where every future session starts — recent encounters, quick actions, and your unread messages are all one click away.

![Noctune dashboard showing welcome banner, recent encounters list, and new encounter button](https://placecats.com/900/520)
