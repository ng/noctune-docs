# Getting Started

This guide covers creating your account and landing on the dashboard for the first time. You can record from your computer's microphone, upload an audio file, or use the iOS app.

## 1. Sign up or sign in

Visit the sign-in page and either create an account or log in with an existing one. Email/password, Google, and Apple are all supported.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/screenshots/getting-started/sign-in.webp"
  alt="Noctune sign-in page showing email, Google, and Apple sign-in options"
  url="app.noctune.ai/sign-in"
/>

> **Tip:** If you signed up through your practice, use the same email your admin invited.

## 2. Accept the terms

The first time you sign in, you'll be asked to accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. You only do this once per account.

![Accept terms screen with checkboxes for Terms of Service and Privacy Policy](https://placecats.com/900/400)

## 3. Land on the dashboard

After accepting terms you're dropped on the Dashboard. This is where every future session starts — the selected day's encounters, recent messages, work awaiting review, and the recording shortcut are all one click away.

  src="/screenshots/dashboard/overview.webp"

  url="app.noctune.ai/dashboard"
