# Dashboard

The Dashboard is your landing page. It surfaces the things you come back for most often: starting a new encounter, checking recent ones, and seeing if anyone has messaged you.

![Full dashboard view with welcome banner, quick upload panel, recent encounters, and messages inbox preview](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## Quick actions

The **New Encounter** button in the top right opens the same encounter drawer as the pill-shaped CTA in the sidebar. From there you can record with your browser microphone, drop or browse for audio files, choose a patient, and select SOAP and discharge-note templates.

![Close-up of the New Encounter pill button in the dashboard header](https://placecats.com/700/200)

## Recent encounters

Your five most recent encounters appear in a compact list. Click any row to jump straight into the transcript viewer and SOAP editor.

![Recent encounters list with patient name, date, and status for each row](https://placecats.com/900/420)

## Messages preview

A preview of your latest unread vet-to-vet messages. Click through to the full [Messages](/messages) inbox.

![Messages inbox preview panel showing three unread messages with sender and subject](https://placecats.com/900/320)

## Record or upload

The New Encounter audio area is both a recording control and a drop zone. Choose **Record from web** to use your microphone, or drop or browse for an existing recording. You can combine multiple recordings and uploaded files in one encounter.

![Audio file upload panel with drag-and-drop zone](https://placecats.com/900/360)
