# New Encounter

Noctune records on your **iOS device** — your phone is the microphone. The web app is for reviewing and editing. From the web, you'll either upload an existing recording or initiate a visit that syncs from your phone.

![New Encounter entry screen with Upload and "Open on iOS" options](https://placecats.com/900/500)

## Recording on iOS

1. Open the Noctune iOS app and tap **Record**.
2. Select the patient (or create one on the fly — see [Patients](/patients)).
3. Pick a [template](/templates) if you want the SOAP note formatted a specific way.
4. Talk through the appointment normally — no scripts, no trigger words.
5. Tap **Stop** when the appointment ends. The encounter uploads automatically and appears in the web app.

![iOS recording screen with elapsed timer and Stop button](https://placecats.com/900/460)

> **Tip:** You can pause and resume on iOS — pauses don't add to the audio file, so there's no dead air in the transcript.

## Uploading audio from the web

If you already have a recording — a phone voice memo, dictaphone file, or audio from another scribe tool — upload it directly from the web. See [Supported Formats & Limits](/reference/supported-formats) for what's accepted.

![Upload dialog showing supported audio formats and file selection area](https://placecats.com/900/420)

## What happens next

Once audio is submitted, Noctune transcribes it and drafts a SOAP note — typically in under a minute. When it's ready the encounter moves from **Processing** to **Draft** and you can open it for review.

![Encounter card in Processing state with progress indicator](https://placecats.com/700/220)
