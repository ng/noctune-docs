# Review & Edit

Open any encounter to see the transcript and SOAP note side by side. This is where you'll spend most of your time in Noctune.

![Encounter detail page with transcript on the left, SOAP note editor on the right, and action bar along the top](https://placecats.com/1000/560)

## Transcript and audio

The transcript is segmented by speaker turn with timestamps. Click any segment to jump the audio player to that moment — useful for double-checking a dosage or a breed name that the AI got wrong.

![Transcript segment with timestamp and speaker label, with the audio player cued to that moment](https://placecats.com/900/320)

## Editing the SOAP note

The SOAP editor is free-form — edit anything you want. Section headers (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) render as markdown.

![SOAP note editor showing Subjective, Objective, Assessment, and Plan sections](https://placecats.com/900/520)

### Regenerate

Not happy with the draft? Click **Regenerate** to produce a new version from the same transcript. You can optionally switch templates before regenerating.

![Regenerate button and template picker in the action bar](https://placecats.com/900/180)

> **Heads up:** Regenerating replaces the current note. Copy anything you want to keep first.

### Append audio

If the visit continued after you stopped recording, use **Append Audio** to add more audio to the same encounter. The transcript extends and the SOAP note is regenerated from the full recording.

![Append audio dialog with drag-and-drop zone and note about transcript being extended](https://placecats.com/800/380)

## Exporting

When the note is final:

* **Copy** — copies to clipboard for pasting into your PIMS
* **Export discharge notes** — a client-safe version for the pet owner
* **Download PDF** — a full-fidelity archive

![Action bar close-up showing Copy, Export, and Download buttons](https://placecats.com/900/160)
