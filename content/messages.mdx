# Messages

Messages has two jobs in Noctune: it's an inbox for vet-to-vet threads, and it's the home of **the Nest** — where pet-owner replies to your emails land, tied back to the encounter they're about.

![Messages inbox with filter tabs for Vet threads and Nest threads, plus a flagged filter](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## The Nest

When you email a discharge note or follow-up from an encounter, you pick how replies route. Choose **Nest** and replies come back to `{encounter-id}@replies.noctune.ai` — they appear as a new thread in Messages, tied to the encounter they reference.

- **Full reference:** [Messaging & Email Relay](/reference/messaging)
- **Why it matters:** the client's follow-up question arrives in context — you (or another vet) sees the original visit alongside the reply.

![Nest thread view with encounter context sidebar showing patient, date, and SOAP summary](https://placecats.com/900/520)

## Noctune Nest vs. no reply vs. personal email

Short version:

- **Noctune Nest** — replies return to the encounter or your Noctune inbox without exposing your real address. Included in subscription.
- **Noctune (no reply)** — a one-way message. The recipient cannot reply.
- **Your personal email** — replies go straight to your personal inbox, and the recipient sees your address.

See [Messaging & Email Relay](/reference/messaging) for the full comparison.

## Vet-to-vet threads

The other half of the inbox is vet-to-vet correspondence for referrals and consults. Threads group messages between you and another vet (or a whole team).

![Vet-to-vet thread with alternating messages, referral context, and SOAP attachment](https://placecats.com/900/480)

## Starting a new message

Choose **New Message** in the top right. The composer opens without leaving your current page. Add one or more recipients, choose how replies should route, then enter a subject and message.

![New message composer with recipient picker, sender route, template picker, subject, and body](https://placecats.com/900/480)

### Use an email template

Choose **Templates** in the composer to search your saved [Email Templates](/email-templates). Inserting a template fills an empty subject and appends the body without replacing anything you already wrote. An **Undo** action is available immediately after insertion.

Placeholders such as the patient's name are filled from the recipient's patient record. Placeholders that cannot be filled appear in amber with an explanation. You can correct the recipient or patient record, go back and edit the message, or explicitly choose **Send anyway**; in that case the recipient sees the placeholder text.

### Reply to a thread

Open a thread and choose **Reply**. Replies use the same full-size composer, template picker, placeholder checks, and sender-routing options as a new message.

### Attachments

Attachments appear on the message that included them. Select an attachment to download it. If a thread contains several messages, expand a collapsed message to see its sender, recipients, timestamp, and attachments.

## Flagged filter

The **Flagged** filter surfaces encounters where [Noctune Sentinel](/reference/sentinel) detected hostility during the visit. Use this when you need to look back on a difficult client.

![Messages inbox with Flagged tab active, showing encounters with hostile-visit indicators](https://placecats.com/900/440)

## Notifications

You get an email for new messages if that's enabled under [Notifications settings](/settings/notifications). The in-app badge updates in real time.

![Sidebar showing the Messages item with an unread count badge](https://placecats.com/600/240)
