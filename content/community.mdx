# Community

The Community section is where vets share work with each other. Right now that's the Template Library — SOAP templates other vets have published, ranked by what the community has found successful.

![Community Template Library page with template cards, filter chips, and search bar](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## Browsing templates

Every public template shows who wrote it, how many vets have liked and forked it, and what kind of visit it's meant for. Click a card to see the full template body and any comments.

![Grid of community template cards with title, author, like and fork counts, and category tag](https://placecats.com/900/480)

## Community-driven SOAP examples

Templates in the library are real, working SOAPs — they're the prompts other vets are actually using to generate notes day-to-day. The like count on each card is the community's vote on what works: a high-like template has produced clean, useful SOAPs across many practices and visit types.

Use likes to find proven starting points instead of writing from scratch. Sort by **Most liked** to see what's working across the community, or filter by visit type when you want a successful template for a specific scenario (wellness, dental, end-of-life, etc.).

![Community template detail page with a heart icon, like count, and fork button](https://placecats.com/900/440)

### Liking a template

Tap the heart on any community template to like it. Your like is public — it counts toward the template's score and signals to the author that their work is useful. Tap again to unlike.

## Forking

**Fork** copies a community template into your own **My Templates**. From there, edit it however you want — the original is untouched.

![Fork button on a community template detail page with confirmation dialog](https://placecats.com/800/400)

## Comments

Each template has a comments thread — ask the author questions, suggest changes, or share how you use it.

![Comments thread under a community template with author replies](https://placecats.com/900/440)

## Reporting

If you see a template that's inaccurate or inappropriate, use the three-dot menu → **Report**. We review reports and act on them.

![Report dialog with reason selector and optional notes field](https://placecats.com/800/360)

> Sharing your own templates back to the community is coming soon.
