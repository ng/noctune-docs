# Supported Formats & Limits

What Noctune accepts when you upload or record audio.

## Accepted audio formats

Any of these work without transcoding on your end:

- **MP3** — most common
- **M4A** (AAC in MP4 container) — iPhone voice memos
- **AAC** (raw)
- **WAV** (PCM / Linear16)
- **FLAC**
- **Ogg**, **Opus**
- **WebM** (audio track)
- **MP4** (audio-only)
- **MP2**

If you have an unusual source, try it — Deepgram reports support for 100+ codec variants. If an upload rejects, transcode to MP3 or M4A and retry.

## Image and document attachments

Not yet supported. Messaging threads accept text only; discharge notes are text with optional PDF export.

## Browser recording

Live recording in the browser uses `MediaRecorder` and produces **WebM/Opus** (Chrome, Edge) or **MP4/AAC** (Safari, some iOS browsers). You don't need to configure anything — Noctune handles both.
