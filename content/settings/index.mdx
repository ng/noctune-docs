# Settings

Settings has three tabs for personal identity, billing access, and notification defaults. Team membership and invitations live under [Practice](/practice).

## Account

Manage the display name shown in messages, the personal Noctune inbox handle, and the **Auto-finish on copy or send** encounter preference.

  src="/screenshots/settings/account.webp"

See [Account](/settings/account).

## Billing & Notes

Review the current plan, included capabilities, notes used this month, remaining allowance, add-ons, available packages, and recent activity.

See [Billing & Notes](/settings/billing).

## Notifications

Review the current email and push defaults for encounters, Messages, billing, and product updates, plus the displayed quiet-hours window.
