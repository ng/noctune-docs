# Settings

Settings is split into three tabs. Pick the area you need.

![Settings page with tabs for Account, Billing & Credits, and Notifications](https://placecats.com/960/420)

## In this section

- [Account](/settings/account) — profile, email, password
- [Billing & Credits](/settings/billing) — plans, usage, payment methods, promo codes
- [Notifications](/settings/notifications) — email and in-app preferences

> Looking for team management? That lives under [Practice](/practice), not Settings.
