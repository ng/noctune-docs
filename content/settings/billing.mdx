# Billing & Credits

Everything about what you pay and what you get. If you're on a team plan, only admins see this tab.

![Billing tab showing current plan, usage meter, payment methods, and credit balance](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## Current plan

The plan card shows what you're on and when it renews. Upgrade or downgrade any time — changes prorate to the next bill.

![Current plan card with tier name, price, renewal date, and Change Plan button](https://placecats.com/900/360)

## Usage

A meter shows how many encounters (or minutes, depending on plan) you've used this period. When you're close to the limit, we'll email you.

![Usage meter with encounters used vs. total for the billing period](https://placecats.com/900/260)

## Payment methods

Add a card, remove an old one, or change which card is the default.

![Payment methods table with card brand, last four digits, expiry, and default toggle](https://placecats.com/900/360)

## Credits and promo codes

Promo codes apply credits to your balance — credits draw down before your card is charged. Enter a code in the **Apply code** box.

![Credit balance display with Apply Promo Code input](https://placecats.com/900/260)

## Invoices

Scroll down for invoice history. Every charge has a downloadable PDF receipt.

![Invoice history table with date, amount, status, and download icon per row](https://placecats.com/900/400)
