# Notifications

Control when Noctune emails you and when it pings you in-app.

![Notifications settings tab with toggles for encounter ready, messages, weekly summary, and product updates](https://placecats.com/960/540)

## Email notifications

Each event has its own toggle. Turning everything off is fine — you'll still see in-app badges, you just won't get email.

![Email notification toggle list with per-event switches](https://placecats.com/900/480)

Common events:

* **Encounter ready** — your SOAP draft is finished processing
* **New message** — someone sent you a vet-to-vet message
* **Weekly summary** — recap of encounters and activity
* **Product updates** — new features and changes

## In-app notifications

In-app notifications (the badges in the sidebar) are always on. They're the source of truth — email just mirrors them.

## Digest timing

Weekly summary sends on Sunday evenings in your local timezone. You can turn it off without affecting any other emails.
